We live busy lives

Vote where and when you want

These days you can top up your phone on the go,send friends money,and shop 24/7. So why can't we vote with as much freedom as carry out other tasks. Thats what we are trying to change here at Uvote.


The way currently vote is by no means the most efficient way to vote, we live busy lives, maybe you can't make it on the given day, or maybe you feel you don't have enough information to make an educated decision. Uvote wants to pull all these problems in to one place and provide a useful easy to use service for both candidates and voters to share information, discuss issues and vote.


We believe that voting should be as easy as checking your email, or tweeting your friends. Voting carries many stereotypes, like everyone we also struggle to understand the value of our votes. By using Uvote, we hope to break these barriers and improve communication between and voters and candidates.


We hope you feel the same
